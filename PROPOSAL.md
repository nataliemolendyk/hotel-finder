## What I'm building:
I am building a website that finds hotels based on the user's flight destination and sorts the hotels by different user preferences.

## Which API I'm using:
I am using the Google Flights API from SerpAPI https://serpapi.com/google-flights-api and the Booking Scraper from Apify https://apify.com/voyager/booking-scraper.

## Why I chose this:
I chose these APIs because I am interested in travelling. A main factor of travelling is finding affordable hotels or close hotels to major cities where airports usually are. In this data, I am the most interested in the hotel information, reviews/ratings, and hotel surroundings. I am also interested in the destination of flights in the flight API.

## Core features:
- Take the user's inputed departing and arriving locations.
- Find hotels near the destination.
- Sort hotels by preferences like reviews/ratings, hotel surroundings, room prices, and features.
